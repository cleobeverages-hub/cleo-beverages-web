03 — Purification

Six precise stages.
Zero shortcuts.

  1. 01

    Multi-grade filtration

    Layered sand and gravel beds strip sediment, turbidity and suspended solids at the source.

  2. 02

    Activated carbon

    Premium coconut-shell carbon removes chlorine, odours and organic compounds for a clean profile.

  3. 03

    Micron filtration

    1µ and 5µ cartridges polish the water, catching the finest residual particles before RO.

  4. 04

    RO purification

    High-pressure reverse osmosis membranes reduce TDS, heavy metals and dissolved impurities.

  5. 05

    UV sterilization

    Calibrated ultraviolet exposure neutralises bacteria, viruses and microbial life — silently, chemically inert.

  6. 06

    Ozonation

    A final ozone polish gives every bottle long-life freshness and a crisp, balanced finish.
