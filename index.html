02 — Purification

The Cleo
Treatment Process.

  1. 01

    Multi-Grade Filtration

    Advanced physical filtration to strictly eliminate suspended solids and turbidity at the source.

  2. 02

    Activated Carbon Filtration

    Premium coconut-shell carbon removes chlorine, color, and organic compounds for a perfectly clean profile.

  3. 03

    RO Purification

    High-pressure Reverse Osmosis membranes effectively reduce heavy metals and dissolved impurities.

  4. 04

    UV Sterilization

    Calibrated ultraviolet exposure neutralizes all microbial life safely and effectively without chemicals.

  5. 05

    Ozonation

    A final ozone polish guarantees long-life freshness, sanitizes the bottle, and provides a crisp finish.
